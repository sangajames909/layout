The UEFA champions league

This league, top 4 different teams across Europe in diffrent leagues are selected to play against each other. The winner collects trophy and huge amount of money. They are placed in diffrent groups where in each group consist of 4 team.

The Premier league

This is played is England. 20 teams play against each for a trophy. The team with most points in a season collects the trophy. It is well known to be one of the best league in the world and it is the most followed league. The bottom 3 clubs are relagated back to the championship.

The Europa leage

Just like the UEFA champions league, this league is also played across Europe. Teams that have been knocked out of the UEFA champions league play in this league. And some teams that have failed to qualify for the champions league play in this league.

The UEFA Super Cup

The EUFA super cup is played between a team that has won the UEFA champions league and the UEFA Europa league.
